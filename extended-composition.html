Video Game Extended Composition

A composition for a video game, taking inspiration from old chiptune and 8-bit sonic landscapes, and fusing them with audio recording transformations and modern synthesisers.

About This Work

The composition was designed as a hypothetical playthrough of a theorised video game. I drafted visuals as well as a storyline, which is detailed in my report, which can be sent if requested. It uses a combination of sound manipulation, transfromation as well as lots of electronic drum beats. I sonically modify my voice, as well as flute and trumpet, to create a glitch-like, underwater soundscape.
